MICROWAVES

CHARACTERISTICS

Frequency range: 109 to 1012 Hertz | Wavelength: 0.3 meters to 0.3 millimeters

EFFECTS

Microwaves are probably best known for their dielectric heating abilities. While this isn't unique to microwaves, their human-scale size makes them the best choice. Dielectric heating occurs when the fluctuating charge from an electromagnetic field forces a polar molecule like water to flip back and forth to match charges, rapidly moving the molecules and causing the material to heat up.
